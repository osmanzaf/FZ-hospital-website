What is the price of Hydrafacial in Islamabad? +

Is laser hair removal safe for Pakistani skin? +
Yes. We use the Soprano Titanium Triple Wave machine which is clinically proven safe for darker South Asian skin tones including Pakistani skin. The continuous cooling system protects the skin surface throughout. Prices start from Rs. 1,500 for small areas up to Rs. 20,000 for full body.
How much does PRP treatment cost and how many sessions are needed? +
PRP for hair or face is Rs. 6,000 per session. PRGF / Lysate Therapy is Rs. 15,000 per session. For hair loss, we recommend 4–6 sessions spaced 3–4 weeks apart. Visible improvement typically begins from the 3rd session. Maintenance sessions every 6 months sustain long-term benefits.
What is the Pico Laser price in Islamabad? +
PICO Laser (without carbon gel) for the face is Rs. 4,000 per session. PICO Laser for hands or feet is Rs. 3,000. Carbon Peel Laser (Black Doll Facial for glow) is Rs. 6,500. Carbon Peel for hands/feet is Rs. 6,000. Results are visible from 2–4 sessions for most pigmentation concerns.
